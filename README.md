# Motor-Insurance-Project
 Here we have dataset of an insurance company which contain vehicle brands, their usage, premium amount, claim amount along with their policy opened and policy closed date.
# Have analysed this dataset various parameteres like;
  the best performing and worse performing year
  the most profitable and loss making brand 
  the most profitable vehicle as per usage
  the vehicle type taking highest number of policies but have worse performing for business point of view
# Defined a KPI i.e. Premium/Claim which tells us the profitability of the business
# Developed a Power BI dashboard along with business insights.
